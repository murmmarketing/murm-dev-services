Frequently asked

How does pricing work?

Fixed pricing per project. I scope the work, give you a number, and that’s what you pay. No hourly billing, no scope creep surprises.

One-time or subscription — which is better?

The one-time option (€750 + €75/mo) is cheaper over 3+ years if you rarely update your site. The subscription (€250 + €150/mo) is better if you want unlimited text edits, regular updates, and someone on call for 2 hours of larger changes each quarter. Lower upfront cost, all-inclusive. 12-month minimum, then month-to-month.

What’s your turnaround?

Websites: 3–5 days. AI automation: 1–2 weeks. Custom web apps: 3–6 weeks depending on scope. I’ll give you an exact timeline before we start.

Can I start with a small project?

Yes. A website build or single automation workflow is a great way to work together before committing to a larger app project.

What if I need ongoing development?

The monthly retainer gives you a developer on call. Bug fixes, new features, automation tweaks — all included. Cancel anytime.
