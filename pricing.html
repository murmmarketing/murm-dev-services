Frequently asked about pricing

Why fixed pricing instead of hourly?

Fixed pricing means you know exactly what you're paying before we start. No surprise invoices, no incentive for me to drag out the work. I scope the project, quote it, and deliver it. If it takes me longer than expected, that's my problem, not yours.

What's the payment process?

50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. For projects over €2,000, we can split into milestones. I invoice via email with bank transfer or PayPal. Dutch clients can pay by iDEAL.

Do you offer discounts?

For long-term partnerships or multiple projects, yes. For a single project, the quoted price is what it is. I don't do discounts for "exposure" or promises of future work.

What if I need changes after delivery?

The post-launch support window (7 or 14 days depending on the package) covers any reasonable adjustments. After that, changes are billed at my hourly rate (€65/hr) or we agree on a maintenance package starting at €150/month.

Can I pay in installments?

For projects over €2,000, yes. We can split into 3 milestones: 40% upfront, 40% at mid-point review, 20% on delivery. Email me to discuss.

Do you charge VAT?

I'm VAT-registered in the Netherlands (KVK 99702738). Dutch and EU-based businesses with a valid VAT number get reverse-charged (no VAT on invoice). Private individuals and non-EU clients are billed at 21% VAT where applicable.
