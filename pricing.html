Frequently asked

How does pricing work?

Fixed pricing per project. I scope the work, give you a number, and that’s what you pay. No hourly billing, no scope creep surprises.

One-off or subscription — which is right for me?

Pay once for a website you own outright (from €600 to €5,900 depending on scope). Or subscribe for €250 setup + €150/mo — all-inclusive, with unlimited text edits and 2 hours/quarter for larger changes. 12-month minimum, then month-to-month.

Why are your delivery times so short?

Fixed scope, tight deliverables, no feature creep. The scope is locked before work starts, so every hour goes into shipping, not redefining the project.

What is the €19.95 maintenance pack?

Hosting + maintenance. Vercel hosting, SSL, security patches, dependency updates, and uptime monitoring. Auto-added to every build unless you decline. No content edits included — that’s what the €150/mo Care plan is for.

How does the Shopify Starter package work?

€1,100, delivered in one week. You get the Dawn theme with brand customization, 20 products imported and styled, collections and product pages, cart and checkout configured, plus payment and shipping setup. A Shopify monthly subscription is separate.

Can I start with a small project?

Yes. A one-pager or Shopify Starter is a great way to work together before committing to a larger app or e-commerce build.

What if I need ongoing development?

The Strategic partner retainer (€500+/mo) gives you a developer on call with weekly sync, unlimited small edits, and scope-locked feature development. Cancel anytime.
