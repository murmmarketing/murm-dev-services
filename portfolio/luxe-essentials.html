A letter from our founder

I started Luxe Essentials in a small studio along the Prinsengracht, inspired by something beautifully simple: Amsterdam's water. The canals, the rain, the soft mineral-rich tap water that Dutch women have quietly credited for generations. I wanted to build a skincare line that honoured that relationship between water and skin -- formulas grounded in hydration science, stripped of everything unnecessary, and made to work with your skin's own biology. Every bottle we produce is a small act of faith in less.

-- Emma, Founder
