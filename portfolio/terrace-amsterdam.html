A kitchen built on relationships

Terrace started in 2019 with a simple idea: cook what grows nearby. Our menu changes with the seasons because our farmers' fields do. Every supplier is within 80km of Amsterdam.

We don't have a walk-in freezer. What arrives in the morning is on your plate by evening. That's not a gimmick — it's just how we think food should work.
