Our Story

Born in Rotterdam.
Tested in the Veluwe.

The Netherlands doesn't get dramatic weather. It gets persistent weather. Sideways rain that finds every gap. Winds that turn a flat landscape into a wind tunnel. Grey mornings that blur into greyer afternoons. We don't design for summit days and bluebird skies. We design for the other 340 days of the year.

Noorvik started in a garage workshop in Rotterdam-Noord, where two industrial designers got tired of outdoor gear that looked great in a showroom but failed on a Tuesday commute across the Erasmusbrug. Every piece we make is tested in the Veluwe forests, on North Sea beaches, and on the daily bike ride to work. If it can handle a Dutch winter, it can handle anything.
