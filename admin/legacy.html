Objection handling

You absolutely can. The question is whether your time is better spent running your business or debugging Conversions API deduplication. I do this every day — it takes me 2 days, it’ll take you 2 weeks.

Totally fair. I’ll send you the audit report so you can see the exact issues. No pressure. But I am only taking on 1 new project right now, so if you do want to move forward, sooner is better.

My current dev can do it

Great — forward them the audit report. If they fix everything on there, you don’t need me. But if it’s been 6 months and those issues are still there, that tells you something.

How do I know it will work?

I measure before and after: LCP, conversion rate, bounce rate. Real numbers from your analytics. If the metrics don’t improve, I’ll keep working until they do — no extra charge.

Can you guarantee results?

I can guarantee the work — every issue in the audit will be fixed. I can’t guarantee a specific revenue number because that depends on your traffic, product, and ads. What I can tell you is that every store I’ve optimized saw measurable improvement.
