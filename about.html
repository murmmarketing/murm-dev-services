About Marius

Shopify & Next.js developer — Leiden, Netherlands

I'm a full-stack developer based in Leiden, Netherlands. I build e-commerce stores and custom web applications for brands that need more than a template. Every project is built by me personally — no outsourcing, no junior devs, no handoffs. That means faster delivery, direct communication, and someone who actually cares about the result.

I work with clients across Europe, the UK, the US, and beyond. Most projects start within three days of the first call and ship in under two weeks.

What I do

My day-to-day work falls into three buckets:

  • Shopify stores — new builds, theme customization, store rebuilds, and speed optimization for stores that aren't converting
  • Custom Next.js applications — e-commerce, B2B portals, and internal tools built on Next.js, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS
  • Tracking & analytics — Meta Pixel, GA4, Klaviyo, and Conversions API setup so paid ads can actually optimize

I don't do WordPress, I don't do hourly billing, and I don't take on projects I can't ship myself. The stack stays modern (Shopify Liquid, Next.js, Supabase, Tailwind) and the scope stays honest.

Recent work

The most public project is MurmReps — a multi-currency Next.js storefront managing 15,000+ products with Klaviyo, Meta Pixel, and GA4 integration. Built end-to-end on Next.js and Supabase. Most other client work is under NDA, but the homepage project section covers what I can share.

Why fixed pricing

I don't bill by the hour. Hourly billing creates the wrong incentives for everyone — the developer is rewarded for being slow, and the client never knows what the final number will be. Every project I take on is quoted as a fixed price up front, with a clear scope and a clear deliverable. If something takes me longer than expected, that's my problem, not the client's.
