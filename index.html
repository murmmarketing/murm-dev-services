Do you work with international clients?

Yes. I work with clients across Europe, the UK, US, and beyond. All communication is in English or Dutch. Timezone differences have never been an issue.

What's your payment process?

50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. For projects over €2,000, we can split into milestones. I invoice via email with bank transfer or PayPal.

Can you help with ongoing maintenance?

Yes. After the 14-day post-launch support, I offer monthly maintenance packages starting at €150/month for updates, fixes, and small improvements.

What if I need changes after launch?

The 14-day support window covers any reasonable adjustments. After that, changes are billed at my hourly rate or we agree on a maintenance package.

Do you build on WordPress?

No. I specialize in Shopify for e-commerce and Next.js + Supabase for custom applications. These are modern, fast, and scalable — no bloated plugins or security headaches.

Can you handle my Meta/Google ads too?

I handle the technical setup — Pixel, Conversions API, GA4, tracking. For ongoing ad management I can recommend trusted partners.
