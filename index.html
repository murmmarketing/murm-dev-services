03 · How it works

Four weeks, call to production.

Week 0 — Call

A sharp conversation.

Free 30 minutes. I ask the hard questions. You decide if I'm the right fit — no pitch deck.

Week 1 — Scope

Fixed, in writing.

Scope and price locked before any code. If it changes, we re-scope in writing, on paper.

Week 2–3 — Build

Weekly Loom.

Staging link from day three. Weekly progress updates by Loom. You see the product, not status reports.

Week 4 — Ship

Production.

Deploy. Handover doc, source access, optional retainer for whatever comes next.
